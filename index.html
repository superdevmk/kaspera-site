Enterprise logistics

TradeFlow — Customs & Logistics Command Center

TradeFlow modernizes outdated customs and logistics desktop systems into a responsive, premium web platform for freight forwarders, shipping agencies and trade companies. One unified workspace for declarations, shipment cases, documents, validation workflows and operational analytics.

Unified dashboard

Real-time overview of open declarations, cleared shipments, import/export/transit volumes and weekly trends.

Shipment case management

Full registry with case types, senders, receivers, status tracking and detailed case views.

3-step case creation

Guided workflow for declaration setup, economic operator data, shipment details and document uploads.

Declaration workflows

5-step declaration builder with transport, goods, financial data, items, documents and validation engine.

Analytics & reports

Operational visibility through statistics, reports and live timeline for customs clearance tracking.

Knowledge base & tools

Built-in guides, workspace settings and assistant dock for day-to-day logistics operations.
